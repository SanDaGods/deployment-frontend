Data Privacy Notice:

Rizal Technological University is dedicated to protecting your privacy information as required by Data Privacy Act of 2012.

Documents Required for Initial Screening:

1. Diploma or Transcript of Records (TOR)

2. 1x1 ID Picture

3. Resume

4. Birth Certificate

5. Barangay Clearance or NBI Clearance or Passport

6. Certificate of Employment (Notarized)

7. Certificates of Training

8. Other supporting documents that qualify your application in the ETEEAP Program.

For unemployed applicants, submit one of the following:

• Income Tax Return (ITR)

• SSS

• PhilHealth

• Pag-IBIG records
