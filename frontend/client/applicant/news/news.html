ETEEAP Provides Opportunities for Professionals

Date: January 2023

According to the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), the Expanded Tertiary Education Equivalency and Accreditation Program (ETEEAP) continues to provide opportunities for professionals to earn academic degrees based on work experience and prior learning. This program, highlighted in recent news, aims to make higher education more accessible for working individuals, especially those who cannot attend regular classes due to career demands. Institutions like Rizal Technological University actively participate in the program, offering flexible learning options and ensuring quality education through strict accreditation processes. ETEEAP plays a vital role in addressing the educational needs of the workforce, fostering career advancement and professional growth in the Philippines (CHED, 2023).

RTU Joins the Expansion of ETEEAP Programs

Date: July 2023

Rizal Technological University (RTU) announced its active participation in the nationwide expansion of the Expanded Tertiary Education Equivalency and Accreditation Program (ETEEAP). This initiative, spearheaded by CHED, aims to increase the number of accredited universities offering the program. RTU will focus on providing opportunities for professionals in education, engineering, and business fields, ensuring high standards of quality and accessibility for learners balancing work and education (RTU, 2023).
